Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What do our stores sell?

A: Our stores sell used or second hand goods that have been donated so that the profits made can help us aid those in need.

Q: What are our values as a charity?

A: We believe in Integrity compassion, respect, diversity, and collaboration

Q: How many people are we helping?

A: We have helped 100,000 meals for the hungry, 2,000 beds for the homeless, 5,000 to 8,000 food vouchers, 1,000 people with assistance in finding employment and Refuge to 500 victims of abuse just to name a few.

Q: Who receives the donations?

A: Anyone in need. The Salvation Army is committed to assisting all people without regard to nationality, race, belief, sexuality, ability, or judgement of behaviour.
