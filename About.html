What the Salvation Army is all about

The Salvation Army is a Christian Charity Organization that operates on an international level all across the world. We have 1,650,000 members working in over 127 countries, each of them working to make the world a better place for the future.

The Salvation Army has existed in Australia for over 130 years and we design programs that benefit Australian's that are effected by Homelessness or disease.

We at the Salvation Army genuinely care about the wellbeing of our country and those who live in it.

Our beleif is that with your help we can give hope where its needed most.
